Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

+

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. Fill in your details including your name, email address, and password. Once submitted, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your account and you're all set!

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise customers, we also offer invoicing options. Contact our sales team for more information on enterprise billing.

How do I cancel my subscription?

+

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account and go to "Account Settings" > "Subscription". Click on "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. We're sorry to see you go and welcome your feedback on how we could improve.

What features are included in the free plan?

+

Our free plan includes basic features such as basic exercises .

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. We maintain regular security audits and are compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. For more details, please review our Security Policy.

Why aren't my workout progress and completed exercises syncing across devices?

+

This issue may occur if you're not logged into the same account on all devices or if there's a temporary internet connectivity problem. Please make sure.
